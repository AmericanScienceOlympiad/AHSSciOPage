About Our Teams

All students attending American High School are welcome to try out for our teams at the beginning of the year. Each year, we choose 15 people for each team. Our tryout process entails trying out for specific events covering all the theory subjects, as well as builds that individuals are interested in. Once teams are selected, each student competes in 3-4 events along with a partner or trio. AHS regularly attends the Golden Gate Invitational, Bay Area Regional Science Olympiad, and Northern California States competitions.
